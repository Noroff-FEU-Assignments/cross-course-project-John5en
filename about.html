About us

Founded in 2022, we are a newly established website that sells games for a competitive and cheap price.


With an emphasis on customer and developer service, we offer a 10% cut for any developer that wants to publish their game on our site.


Don't like the game you purchased? As long as you don't exceed 3 hours of playtime, you are eligible for a refund.
